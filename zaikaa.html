About ZaiKaa

ZaiKaa is an Indian based multinational restaurant chain
founded in 2012 and led by CEO Rajeev Sharma.
As of 2023 Zaikaa has 300 stores all around the world.
ZaiKaa uses olive oil and special spices from farms
Kerela. It is famous for northern and south cusines.
